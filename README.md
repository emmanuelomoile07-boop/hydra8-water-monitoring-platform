# Hydra8 Water Monitoring Platform

Hydra8 is developing an open monitoring and routing coordination platform that supports decentralized neighborhood-scale water infrastructure in climate-vulnerable urban communities.

## Problem

Rapidly growing cities such as Lagos face unreliable access to safe water due to limited centralized infrastructure, rapid population growth, and seasonal flooding disruptions. Communities often depend on fragmented tanker supply systems with little coordination or infrastructure visibility.

## Solution

This platform supports:

- Water storage level monitoring at decentralized hub locations
- Routing coordination for efficient localized water distribution
- Infrastructure visibility across supply nodes
- Climate disruption response coordination during flooding events

The system improves reliability of safe water access for households, schools, and health facilities in underserved urban communities.

## Open Source Commitment

Hydra8 is committed to releasing selected monitoring and coordination components as open-source tools to support partners, governments, and humanitarian actors deploying decentralized water infrastructure across climate-vulnerable regions.

## Status

Architecture development in progress.
Pilot hub deployment planned in Lagos, Nigeria.
